Welcome to Chandrauta Vibes

Chandrauta Vibes is a page dedicated to showcasing the mesmerizing beauty of Chandrauta, a hidden gem in Nepal. Through our videos and posts, we bring the scenic landscapes, culture, and life of this place straight to you.

Owner: Shashwat Gupta

A passionate student and explorer, Shashwat Gupta founded Chandrauta Vibes to share the beauty of his hometown with the world.
